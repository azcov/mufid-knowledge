# Design Tokens — Kostify

## Brand Personality
**Reliable · Organized · Calm · For Indonesian Landlords**
Feels like having an organized assistant for your property.

## Colors
| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Primary | `#B45309` | Warm amber/brown — property, earth, trust |
| Secondary | `#F59E0B` | Gold — value, income |
| Paid | `#10B981` | Green — ✅ sudah bayar |
| Late | `#EF4444` | Red — ❌ nunggak |
| Surface | `#FFFBEB` | Warm cream background |
| Text | `#1C1917` | Near-black |

## Typography
| Role | Font | Weight |
|------|------|--------|
| Headline | Plus Jakarta Sans | 700 |
| Body | Plus Jakarta Sans | 400 |
| Data/numbers | Inter | 600 |

## Visual Style
- **Photography:** Kos buildings, hands with keys, clean room interiors (Indonesian style)
- **Graphics:** Warm amber + white. Property management dashboard style.
- **Avoid:** Luxury real estate aesthetic — this is for modest landlords, not property developers

## Brand Voice
- Empathetic to landlord struggles (awkward reminders, forgotten contracts)
- Practical and no-nonsense
- Frame as "peace of mind" — not just efficiency
- Indonesian, conversational

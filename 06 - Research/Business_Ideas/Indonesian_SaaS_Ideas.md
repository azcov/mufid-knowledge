# Indonesian SaaS Ideas

Distilled from multiple research conversations: "Ide SaaS untuk market Indonesia" (2026-05-02) and "Ide SaaS untuk pasar Indonesia" (2026-05-16).

## Framework: Sweet Spot Criteria
Good opportunity = **ada beberapa pesaing** (demand terbukti) + **belum ada pemain dominan**. Hindari segmen pesaing hampir 0 (market belum terbukti) dan pesaing terlalu banyak (terlalu kompetitif).

| Ide | Potensi | Catatan |
|-----|---------|---------|
| POS + Inventory warung/toko kelontong | ⭐⭐⭐ | Pasar besar, mayoritas masih manual |
| Manajemen kasir multi-cabang franchise F&B | ⭐⭐⭐ | Ada pesaing (Moka, iReap) tapi segmen bisa dikhususkan |
| B2B procurement restoran/kafe dari supplier | ⭐⭐ | Butuh 2-sided marketplace |

| Kasir komunitas/RT/RW | ⭐⭐ | Viral, harga rendah tapi volume bisa besar |

| **Manajemen Pondok Pesantren** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | 26.000+ pesantren, hampir semua manual, pesaing nyaris 0 (risiko: market belum terbukti) |

## Top 3 Rekomendasi untuk Solo Founder
1. **Kost/Kontrakan management** — pain point jelas, self-serve, WA-native, churn rendah → jadi Kontrakan/Kostify
2. **Bengkel directory** — SEO moat, dual monetization (AdSense + subscription) → jadi Bengkelterdekat
3. **HR Payroll/Payslip SME** — recurring, sticky, Indonesia-specific components (BPJS, PPh 21) → jadi SlipGaji

## Monetization Models for Directories
- **AdSense** — passive, scales with traffic
- **Freemium listing** — free basic, paid featured/managed
- **Subscription** — owner pays monthly to manage/boost listing
- **Lead generation** — paid per inquiry/lead
- **Affiliate** — commission on referred business

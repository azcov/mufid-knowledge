# Design Tokens — WaCRM

## Brand Personality
**Smart · Practical · Action-Oriented · For Indonesian Sellers**

## Colors
| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Primary | `#075E54` | WhatsApp dark green — instant recognition |
| Accent | `#25D366` | WhatsApp bright green — CTA |
| Dark | `#111827` | Text |
| Surface | `#F0FDF4` | Light green background |
| Warning | `#F59E0B` | Follow-up due alert |

## Typography
| Role | Font | Weight |
|------|------|--------|
| Headline | Inter | 700–800 |
| Body | Inter | 400–500 |

## Visual Style
- **WhatsApp-adjacent** — green palette creates immediate recognition
- **Screenshots of WA chats** as content (with blurred names) — very relatable
- **Clean dashboard previews** — show the product clearly
- **Avoid:** Corporate CRM look. This is for warung owners, not enterprise sales teams.

## Brand Voice
- Conversational, street-smart seller language
- "Jangan sampai deal kamu kabur cuma karena lupa follow up"
- Talks in terms of money lost/saved, not features
- Bahasa Indonesia informal but professional

# Design Tokens — Suratpedia

## Brand Personality
**Formal · Trustworthy · Official · Indonesian**
Feels like a government-adjacent resource — authoritative but accessible.

## Colors
| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Primary | `#1E3A5F` | Deep navy — formal, official |
| Accent | `#2563EB` | Blue — digital, link color |
| Gold | `#CA8A04` | Document seal/stamp feel |
| Surface | `#F8FAFC` | Clean white-gray |
| Text | `#1E293B` | Dark |

## Typography
| Role | Font | Weight |
|------|------|--------|
| Headline | Merriweather or Playfair Display | 700 (formal, editorial) |
| Body | Inter | 400 |
| Letter preview | Noto Serif Indonesia | 400 (matches formal letter feel) |

## Visual Style
- **Style:** Clean, document-inspired. Think: a clean government website, not a startup.
- **Document previews:** Show actual letter templates as the hero visual
- **Colors:** Navy + white + gold. Feels credible and official.
- **Avoid:** Too casual or trendy — users need to trust this is formal and correct

## Brand Voice
- Authoritative ("Format yang benar untuk surat resmi Indonesia")
- Helpful and practical ("Download langsung, isi nama kamu, selesai")
- No fluff — users come with a task, help them complete it

# Competitor Analysis — IndoDev-ID

| Competitor | Weakness | Our Edge |
|------------|----------|----------|
| **LinkedIn** | Global noise, expensive to post ($300+/post), generic | Tech-only, free to post, Bahasa Indonesia, salary required |
| **Glints** | Mixes tech with retail/logistics/admin | Tech-only — no noise for developers or companies |
| **Kalibrr** | Not tech-specific, no salary enforcement | Same as Glints |
| **Telegram/Discord groups** | Informal, no application flow | Structured profiles, apply tracking, searchable |

## Our Wedge
Salary transparency + tech-only = trust signal developers can't get elsewhere.
At 10K+ devs registered, companies have no choice but to pay for visibility.

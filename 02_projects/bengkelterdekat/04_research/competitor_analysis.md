# Competitor Analysis — Bengkelterdekat

## Competitors
| Competitor | What they do | Our Differentiation |
|------------|-------------|---------------------|
| **Google Maps** | Nearest workshop, generic reviews | Specialist filter; structured specialization tags (kaki-kaki, AC, transmisi) |
| **OLX/Tokopedia Services** | Unstructured ads, no quality signal | Curated directory, admin-verified, active owner profiles |
| **Instagram/Facebook** | Social posts, no discovery intent | Intent-driven search: "I need X right now" |
| **Mamikos/Kos88** | Different vertical (rentals) | Wrong category |

## Indirect Competitors
- WhatsApp/Facebook group asks ("ada yang tau bengkel...") — word of mouth at scale
- Google Maps reviews — generic, can't filter by specialization

## Market Gap
No Indonesian platform lets car owners filter by specialization (kaki-kaki, AC mobil, diesel, body repair) + car brand (Korean, European, Japanese) + price range simultaneously. Google Maps shows proximity; we show qualification.

## Revenue Model Comparison
- Google Maps: free (ads-funded globally, not monetized per workshop in Indonesia)
- OLX: free listing + promoted ads
- Bengkelterdekat: workshop owner subscription (Rp 149K/mo) + AdSense from visitor traffic

## Key Risk
A well-funded player (Tokopedia, GoTo, Garda Oto) could build this. Defense: SEO moat (150+ indexed pages day 1) + direct workshop owner relationships + Indonesian-native product.

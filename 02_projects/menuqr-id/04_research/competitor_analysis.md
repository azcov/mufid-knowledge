# Competitor Analysis — MenuQR

| Competitor | Focus | Weakness | Our Edge |
|------------|-------|----------|----------|
| **GoFood/ShopeeFood** | Delivery (20-30% commission) | Not for dine-in, commission-heavy | Dine-in only, no commission, WhatsApp routing |
| **Moka POS** | Full POS system | Requires hardware, training, monthly fee | No hardware, 15-min setup |
| **Qasir** | POS + inventory | Desktop-heavy, complex | Simple QR menu only |
| **Instagram Highlight as menu** | Free | No ordering flow, photos unstructured | Structured menu + instant order |
| **Printed laminated menu** | Rp 0–50K one-time | Expensive to update, no sold-out toggle | Live updates, no reprint ever |

## Market Opportunity
Post-QRIS, Indonesian food businesses are comfortable with QR technology.
GoFood/ShopeeFood serve delivery; MenuQR serves dine-in direct orders.
Millions of warung, kafe, food stalls remain unserved.

## Revenue Projection
Month 3: 100 outlets → Rp 5.9M MRR
Month 6: 500 outlets → Rp 29.5M MRR
Month 12: 2,000 outlets → Rp 118M MRR
Assumed blended ARPU: Rp 59K/mo

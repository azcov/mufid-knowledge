# Scope & Goals — IndoDev-ID

## Goal
Tech-specific job board for Indonesian developers. Connects Indonesian devs with local startups and global remote-first companies. Free to post, salary transparency enforced, paid boosts for visibility.

## In Scope (MVP — Q3–Q4 2026)
**Developer side (free):** Registration + profile, job search/browse, detail page, one-click apply with profile, application status tracking, auth (email + Google), Bahasa Indonesia + English toggle.

**Company side (paid boosts):**
- Free: 1 active job post, standard placement, basic applicant management
- Featured placement: Rp 299K / 7 days
- Email blast to matched devs: Rp 499K / send
- Candidate search & filter: Rp 199K / month
- Salary range: **required field — publish blocked without it**

**Cold Start:** Manual seeding of 200+ listings pre-launch (curated from company career pages)

## Out of Scope
- Learning platform / courses
- Salary comparison market data tool
- Developer portfolio/reputation system
- Community forum (Phase 3, after 10K+ devs)
- Non-tech job categories — never
- Recruitment agency model

## Success Metrics (Month 6)
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Registered developers | 10,000 |
| Active job listings | 200+ at any time |
| Companies with ≥ 1 paid boost | 50 |
| Developer apply rate | ≥ 30% apply to ≥ 1 job |
| Company return rate | ≥ 40% post 2nd job within 90 days |

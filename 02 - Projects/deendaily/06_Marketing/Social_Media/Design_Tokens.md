# Design Tokens — DeenDaily

## Brand Personality
**Calm · Sincere · Spiritual · Modern Muslim**
Feels like a sincere friend reminding you, not a lecture.

## Colors
| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Primary | `#1B4332` | Deep forest green — Islamic, grounded |
| Gold | `#D4AF37` | Islamic gold — spiritual, warmth |
| Light | `#F0FDF4` | Soft green background |
| Dark | `#0F172A` | Deep dark for text |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Clean |

## Typography
| Role | Font | Weight |
|------|------|--------|
| Headline | Playfair Display or Amiri | 700 (elegant, Islamic feel) |
| Body | Inter | 400–500 |
| Arabic text | Amiri or Scheherazade New | Regular |

## Visual Style
- **Photography:** Mosques, prayer rugs, hands in dua, calm nature (sunrise, water)
- **Graphics:** Deep green + gold. Geometric Islamic patterns as subtle accents.
- **Arabic typography:** Use correctly — calligraphic style for Quranic text
- **Avoid:** Cartoonish Islamic content, overly corporate look

## Brand Voice
- Sincere, not preachy
- Acknowledges struggle — no toxic positivity
- Bilingual OK (English + Bahasa Indonesia)
- Uses terms the audience knows: istiqomah, muhasabah, ibadah, amal

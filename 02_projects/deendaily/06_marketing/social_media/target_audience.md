# Target Audience — DeenDaily

## Primary — Muslim Young Adults (Global, English)
| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Age | 18–35 |
| Location | Indonesia (primary), Malaysia, UK, US, Canada |
| Identity | Practicing Muslim who struggles with consistency |
| Pain | "Semangat habis Ramadan, 2 minggu kemudian balik ke habits lama" |
| Current tool | Mental tracking ("kayaknya tadi sholat dhuha deh") or nothing |
| Trigger | After Ramadan, after a talk, after a life event (nikah, anak lahir) |

## Secondary — Indonesian Muslim Professionals
| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Age | 25–40 |
| Status | Busy working professional, feels deen has slipped |
| Pain | Jadwal padat, sholat sering qadha, merasa jauh dari Allah |
| Language | Bahasa Indonesia |

## Psychographics
- Guilty but motivated — wants to improve, just needs a system
- Responds to: authenticity, Islamic quotes, real talk about spiritual struggle
- Engaged in Muslim communities on Twitter and Instagram
- Likely follows: Muslim lifestyle creators, Islamic scholars on social
- Values: consistency (istiqomah), spiritual growth, community accountability

# Scope & Goals — SlipGaji

## Goal
Indonesian payslip generator SaaS. Set up employees once → generate all payslips as professional PDFs in one click per payroll period. Built specifically for Indonesian salary components.

## In Scope (MVP)
- Employee management with salary structure
- Indonesian components: gaji pokok, tunjangan tetap/tidak tetap, BPJS Kesehatan, BPJS Ketenagakerjaan (JHT, JP, JKK, JKM), PPh 21 TER, overtime
- One-click payslip generation → PDF per employee
- Company logo on PDF
- Salary history per employee
- Xendit subscription billing
- Web-first (HR admins work on desktop)

## Out of Scope (MVP)
- Full HRIS (attendance, leave, performance)
- Salary auto-transfer / bank disbursement (tracks only, doesn't pay)
- Employee self-service portal (Phase 2)
- Mobile app
- SPT Masa / DJP tax filing (shows calculation, doesn't file)
- BPJS portal integration (calculation only)

## Why Personal Brand Matters for This Business

1. **Trust transfer** — people trust the founder before they trust the product. Especially for B2B (SlipGaji, WaCRM, Bengkelterdekat).
2. **Cheaper acquisition** — 10K followers on a founder account = free distribution for every product launch.
3. **Talent magnet** — when it's time to hire, candidates research the founder first.
4. **Investor credibility** — if you ever raise, VCs will Google you.
5. **Indonesian market** — "Made by Mufid" carries weight when the story is right.

## Content Strategy Per Platform

### Instagram — Building in Public + Personal
**Vibe:** Authentic, behind-the-scenes, visual.

| Type | Example |
|------|---------|
| Progress updates | "Week 12: BudgetNest first 10 signups 🎉" |
| Behind the scenes | Screenshot of code, Obsidian vault, Figma design |
| Personal story | "Kerja di GTL siang, bangun startup malem — ini routinenya" |
| Product teaser | "Ini yang lagi gue bangun... (tanpa spoil terlalu banyak)" |
| Milestones | "First Rp 1 juta dari internet 🔥" |
| Learning | "Hal yang gue pelajari dari gagal launch [X]" |

**Posting:** 3–4x/week (Stories daily, Feed 3x/week)

---

### LinkedIn — Professional Credibility
**Vibe:** Thoughtful, career-adjacent, insights.

| Type | Example |
|------|---------|
| Career insight | "4 years building logistics systems at Tokopedia and GoTo — here's what I learned about scale" |
| Founder journey | "Why I'm building 12 products while working a full-time senior engineering job" |
| Tech opinion | "Why I chose Go for all my SaaS backends — and what I'd change" |
| Industry insight | "Indonesian SaaS in 2026: what's working, what's not" |
| Hiring (future) | "Looking for a Go engineer who wants to build for Indonesia" |

**Posting:** 2–3x/week. Long-form text posts perform well on LinkedIn.

---

### Twitter/X — Dev Community + Thoughts
**Vibe:** Raw, quick, developer-native.

| Type | Example |
|------|---------|
| Build log | "Shipped Suratpedia's parser service today. FastAPI → TipTap JSON → PDF. Took 3 days." |
| Tech take | "Indonesian SaaS founders: stop building for global, build for your neighbor first" |
| Salary data | "Go Backend salary in Jakarta 2026 — thread 🧵" (feeds IndoDev-ID) |
| Honest post | "Side project update: 3 products live, 0 paying users. Here's what I'm changing." |

**Posting:** Daily or every 2 days. Quality > quantity.

## Personal Brand Pillars (What Mufid Stands For)

1. **Building for Indonesia** — products for real Indonesian problems, not copies of Western SaaS
2. **Craft over speed** — Go architecture, clean code, production-grade from day one
3. **Solo founder, full transparency** — honest about what works and what doesn't
4. **Day job + indie maker** — showing it's possible to build while employed

# Competitor Analysis — DeenDaily

## Direct Competitors
| Competitor | Focus | Weakness vs DeenDaily |
|------------|-------|----------------------|
| **Muslim Pro** | Prayer times, Quran, qibla | No habit tracking; different product |
| **Tarteel** | Quran memorization AI | Single-feature; no salah/sunnah tracking |
| **Habitica** | Generic gamified habit tracker | No Islamic context, no salah defaults |
| **Streaks (iOS)** | Habit streaks | No Islamic defaults, paid app, iOS only |
| **Productive (app)** | Habit tracker | Generic, no Islamic context, English only |

## Market Gap
No app owns the "daily ibadah check-in loop." Muslim Pro has 100M+ downloads but is a utility (prayer times, Quran reader). Nobody builds the habit layer that connects all pillars of daily Islamic practice.

## Differentiation
1. **Zero setup for core ibadah** — salah + sunnah pre-configured, no form to fill
2. **Honest habit data** — monthly view shows exactly where practice is strong or weak
3. **Bahasa Indonesia** — primary market is Indonesia (240M Muslims)
4. **Rp 16K/mo pricing** — accessible for Indonesian users vs $3-5/mo USD alternatives
5. **Streak psychology** — same mechanic as Duolingo but for ibadah consistency

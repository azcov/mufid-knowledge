# App Store Publishing Costs & Rules

Source: "Biaya publikasi app di app store dan google play" (2026-05-26)

## Costs

| Platform | Cost | Type |
|----------|------|------|
| **Apple App Store** | $99/year | Annual renewal |
| Apple Enterprise Program | $299/year | Internal distribution only |
| **Google Play Store** | $25 | One-time, lifetime |

## App Limits
- Both platforms: **no official limit** on number of apps per account
- No extra fee per additional app

## Duplicate App Rules

### Safe
- Two apps with clearly different purpose and features
- Apps in same category if functionality is distinct
- Many large developers publish multiple apps in health/fitness

### Risky
- App B has overlapping features with App A
- Two accounts owned by same person/entity (explicitly prohibited)
- Apps that appear to "spam" the store

### Risk Levels
| Scenario | Risk |
|----------|------|
| Healthy app (general) + Diet & Meal app (specific) — no overlap | ✅ Safe |
| Healthy app with diet feature + separate Diet & Meal app | ⚠️ Gray zone — risk rejection |
| Two identical apps with different names | ❌ Will be rejected/banned |
| Two accounts for same person to bypass rules | ❌ Ban risk |

## Strategy for Multiple Apps
- Build separate apps per product (not variations of same app)
- Each app should have clearly differentiated core value proposition
- Avoid building App B that duplicates a feature already in App A
- Separate apps = separate accounts only if legally different entities

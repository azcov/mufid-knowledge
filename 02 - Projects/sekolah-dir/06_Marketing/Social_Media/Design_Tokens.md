# Design Tokens — Sekolah-Dir

## Brand Personality
**Trustworthy · Caring · Comprehensive · For Indonesian Parents**
Feels like a knowledgeable older sibling who's been through school selection.

## Colors
| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Primary | `#1D4ED8` | Trustworthy blue — education, reliability |
| Secondary | `#F59E0B` | Warm amber — approachable, family |
| Surface | `#EFF6FF` | Light blue background |
| Text | `#1E293B` | Dark |
| Success | `#10B981` | Accredited / verified badge |

## Typography
| Role | Font | Weight |
|------|------|--------|
| Headline | Plus Jakarta Sans | 700 |
| Body | Plus Jakarta Sans | 400 |
| School names | Inter | 600 |

## Visual Style
- **Photography:** School buildings (Indonesian style), children in uniform, parents visiting schools
- **Style:** Friendly, warm blue + white. Credible but not sterile.
- **Review cards:** Parent quote cards with simple star ratings
- **Avoid:** Generic education stock photos. Indonesian school context only.

## Brand Voice
- Empathetic to parenting anxiety ("Pilih sekolah itu berat — kami bantu")
- Data-driven but human ("4.2/5 dari 47 review orang tua")
- Informative without being overwhelming
- Indonesian, warm but professional

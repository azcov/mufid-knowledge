# Competitor Analysis — WaCRM

| Competitor | Price | Weakness | Our Edge |
|------------|-------|----------|----------|
| **Qontak** | Rp 500K+/mo | WhatsApp API approval required, complex onboarding | No API required at MVP, Rp 99K/mo solo |
| **HubSpot** | Free→$45+/mo | Built for Western sales teams, complex, English | Built for Indonesian SME, simple, Bahasa |
| **Salesforce** | $25+/user/mo | Enterprise complexity, overkill | Same |
| **WhatsApp labels** | Free | Only 20 labels, no reminders, no history | Unlimited tags, reminders, full history |
| **Spreadsheet** | Free | Not searchable, no reminders, breaks with team | Structure + reminders + team access |

## Market Gap
Rp 50K–249K/month tier for Indonesian SMEs is nearly empty. The follow-up reminder framed as "lost sales recovery" (not "organization tool") is the key conversion lever.

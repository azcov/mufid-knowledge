# Target Audience — BudgetNest

## Primary — Young Indonesian Couples
| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Age | 24–35 |
| Status | Newly married or living together, managing shared finances |
| Income | Dual income Rp 8–25M/month combined |
| Location | Urban: Jakarta, Bandung, Surabaya, Tangerang, Bekasi |
| Pain | "Kok uangnya habis ya padahal masih tanggal 20?" |
| Current tool | WhatsApp group, Notes app, or nothing |
| Trigger | First big fight about money, or planning for something (rumah, anak, liburan) |

## Secondary — Young Professionals (Solo)
| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Age | 22–30 |
| Status | First job, first real salary, wants to save |
| Pain | Has no idea where money goes |
| Trigger | Payday passes and account is already low |

## Psychographics
- Embarrassed to admit they don't track spending
- Wants to be "the couple that has it together"
- Sees budgeting as a couple activity — not just one person's job
- Influenced by "financial freedom" content on TikTok/YouTube
- Responds to relatable memes and honest content over corporate advice

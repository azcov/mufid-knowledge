# Design Tokens — Kulitku

## Brand Personality
**Safe · Trustworthy · Science-backed · For Indonesian Mothers**
Feels like a knowledgeable friend who researches everything for you.

## Colors
| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Primary | `#BE185D` | Rose/pink — feminine, skincare |
| Safe green | `#10B981` | ✅ Safe ingredient indicator |
| Warning | `#F59E0B` | ⚠️ Caution ingredient |
| Danger | `#EF4444` | ❌ Avoid indicator |
| Surface | `#FFF1F2` | Soft rose background |
| Text | `#1F2937` | Dark gray |

## Typography
| Role | Font | Weight |
|------|------|--------|
| Headline | Plus Jakarta Sans | 700 |
| Body | Plus Jakarta Sans | 400–500 |
| Ingredient names | JetBrains Mono | 500 (scientific, clinical feel) |

## Visual Style
- **Photography:** Mothers with products, pregnancy, baby skin, skincare routines (clean, natural light)
- **Graphics:** Soft pinks + white. Safety badges (✅ BPOM, ✅ Halal) prominent.
- **Scientific credibility:** Use ingredient names correctly, cite BPOM where possible
- **Avoid:** Overly luxury skincare aesthetic — this is about safety, not glamour

## Brand Voice
- Calm and reassuring ("Kulitku sudah cek — ini yang perlu kamu tahu")
- Science-based, not fear-mongering
- Empathetic to the anxiety of pregnancy
- Clear, simple — not medical jargon

# Target Audience — Suratpedia

## Primary — Anyone Who Needs a Formal Indonesian Letter
| Profile | Needs |
|---------|-------|
| Fresh grad / job seeker | Surat lamaran, surat pengalaman kerja |
| Employee | Surat izin tidak masuk, surat cuti |
| Student | Surat keterangan aktif, surat izin orang tua |
| Business owner | Surat perjanjian, MOU, surat penawaran |
| General public | Surat domisili, surat kehilangan, pernyataan |

**Demographics:** All ages, all over Indonesia. Searched when needed — intent-driven.

## Psychographics
- Has never written this specific letter before
- Wants to look professional / official
- Doesn't want to start from blank — wants a template
- Often in a time crunch (perlu hari ini / besok)
- Bahasa Indonesia formal is tricky — they're not confident they're writing it right

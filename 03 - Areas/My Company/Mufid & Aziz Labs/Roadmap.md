### Team target (5–8 people):

### Products:
- Double down on the 2–3 winners
- Shut down or sell products that never found traction
- Consider raising from Indonesian VC only if needed to accelerate a proven winner

---

## Kill Criteria
A product gets **paused or killed** if after 6 months of being live:
- < 50 paying users, AND
- No clear path to 200 users in the next 3 months

Don't drag products that don't work. Time is the scarcest resource.

---

## Focus Rule (Anti-Pattern to Avoid)
The biggest risk: building 12 products forever and shipping none.

> **One product in users' hands > twelve products in development.**

At any given time, max **2 products in active development** simultaneously. Everything else is in maintenance or queue.

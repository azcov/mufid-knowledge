# Personal Brand — Social Media Strategy

## Platform Strategy

| Platform | Posting | Focus |
|----------|---------|-------|
| **Instagram** | 3–4x/week + Stories daily | Building in public, product updates, personal |
| **LinkedIn** | 2–3x/week | Professional credibility, B2B reach, career insights |
| **Twitter/X** | Daily or every 2 days | Dev community, tech takes, build logs |
| **TikTok** | 2–3x/week (lower priority) | Casual, day-in-the-life, quick demos |

## Content Mix

- 40% Building in public (product progress, learnings)
- 25% Technical / career (insights, opinions)
- 20% Personal / relatable (routine, struggles)
- 15% Promotional (product launches, milestones)

## Weekly Rhythm

| Day | Platform | Content |
|-----|----------|---------|
| Mon | LinkedIn | Insight or career post |
| Tue | Twitter | Dev/build log |
| Wed | Instagram | Behind the scenes / progress |
| Thu | LinkedIn | Tech take or founder story |
| Fri | Twitter | Product update or thread |
| Sat | Instagram | Personal / milestone |
| Sun | Rest / TikTok | Optional casual video |

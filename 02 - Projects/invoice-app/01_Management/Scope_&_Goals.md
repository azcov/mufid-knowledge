# Scope & Goals — Invoice App

## Goal
Zero-subscription invoicing for Indonesian freelancers and small agencies. Replace the Excel + WhatsApp + bank transfer workflow with a branded client payment portal. Revenue from transaction fees only (Wave model for Indonesia).

## In Scope (MVP — Q3–Q4 2026)
- Invoice creation (line items, tax %, flat discount, notes, due date)
- PDF generation (professional template)
- Branded client payment portal (no client account required)
- Signed link access (token-gated, 30-day expiry)
- Payment methods via Xendit/Midtrans: QRIS, VA (BCA/Mandiri/BNI/BRI), GoPay, OVO, Dana, ShopeePay, BNPL, credit card
- Invoice status: draft / sent / viewed / paid / overdue
- Automated overdue reminder to client (Day +3 after due date)
- Basic client/contact address book
- Transaction fee collection (% of invoice value)

## Out of Scope (MVP)
- Accounting / bookkeeping
- Corporate cards / expense management
- Supplier payments / PaperPay Out
- Payroll
- Inventory management
- Mobile app — web-first
- Multi-entity / holding company
- ERP integrations
- e-Meterai (Phase 2)

## Business Model
Transaction fee only — no subscription.
User earns → platform earns. Zero barrier to start.
Fee % TBD: competitive with Paper.id (currently 1.45–1.9% credit card, free VA with conditions).

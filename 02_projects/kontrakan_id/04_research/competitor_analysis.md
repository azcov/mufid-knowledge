# Competitor Analysis — Kontrakan / Kostify

| Competitor | Focus | Weakness | Our Edge |
|------------|-------|----------|----------|
| **Mamikos / Kos88** | Rental marketplace (find kos) | Different product; listing site, not management | We manage existing tenants, not find new ones |
| **Property management software** | 50+ units, agencies | Too complex, too expensive for 8-unit landlord | Built for 1–20 units, Rp 49–99K/mo |
| **Google Sheets** | Free tracking | No reminders, no automation, breaks at 10+ units | Automated reminders, contract tracking |
| **WhatsApp groups** | Free communication | No payment tracking, no contract visibility | Structured data + automation |
| **Buku kas / paper notebook** | Free | Not searchable, no alerts | Dashboard + alerts + history |

## Key Insight
Indonesia has 3–5 million informal landlords. Almost none have digital tools.
The WhatsApp automated reminder (via Fonnte) is the single highest-value feature — landlords avoid awkward manual reminders, leading to consistently late rent. Automation removes the social awkwardness.

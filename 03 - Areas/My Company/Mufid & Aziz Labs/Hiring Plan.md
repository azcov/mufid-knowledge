# Hiring Plan

## Philosophy
Hire for the gap that's blocking revenue, not for the gap that feels uncomfortable.
First hire = the skill you can't do yourself AND is slowing down user growth.

---

## Hire #1 — Product Designer (Rp 5–8M/mo, contract)
**Trigger:** MRR Rp 20–30M. When rough UI is the reason users don't convert.

**What they own:**
- UI/UX for all active products
- Landing pages that convert
- App store screenshots and marketing assets
- Design system shared across products

**Why first:** Mufid can build but not design at market standard.
Engineering quality is high — design is the bottleneck for conversion.

---

## Hire #2 — Growth / Marketing (Rp 5–8M/mo, contract)
**Trigger:** MRR Rp 30–50M. When product is good but nobody finds it.

**What they own:**
- SEO content (critical for all directory products)
- Social media (Instagram, TikTok for Indonesian market)
- Paid ads (Meta, Google) for highest-MRR product
- Community presence (Facebook groups, WhatsApp communities)

**Why second:** All products depend on organic discovery. SEO takes time — start early.

---

## Hire #3 — Backend Engineer Go (Rp 15–25M/mo, full-time)
**Trigger:** MRR Rp 80–100M. When product backlog is too large to ship alone.

**What they own:**
- Feature development on 1–2 products
- On-call for production issues
- Code review + quality

**Profile:**
- 2–4 years Go experience
- Knows PostgreSQL, REST APIs, Docker
- DDD or clean architecture familiarity preferred
- Indonesian, Bandung/remote preferred

---

## Hire #4 — Frontend/Mobile Engineer (Rp 12–20M/mo, full-time)
**Trigger:** MRR Rp 100–150M. When mobile apps need dedicated attention.

**What they own:**
- React Native mobile apps (iOS + Android)
- Next.js frontend features
- Performance and UX polish

---

## Hiring Principles

1. **Contract before full-time** — prove the role needs full-time before committing
2. **Hire character, train skill** — values alignment > perfect CV
3. **No visa/expat hires early** — Indonesian talent only at bootstrap stage (cost + culture)
4. **Remote-first** — Bandung base, full remote OK for right person
5. **Async by default** — no mandatory standup culture, results-based

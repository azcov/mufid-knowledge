# Digital Product Platforms — Indonesia

Source: "Prospek produk digital di Indonesia" (2026-05-30)

## Platform Comparison

| Platform | Best For | Fee | Notes |
|----------|---------|-----|-------|
| **Lynk.id** | Link-in-bio, simple product store | % transaction | Popular for Indonesian creators |
| **Mayar.id** | Creator with audience, subscription | % transaction | Go SDK available (→ [[02 - Projects/go-mayar-sdk/00_Project_Hub|go-mayar-sdk]]) |
| **Scalev** | Course + community bundle | % + monthly | Higher-ticket products |
| **Gumroad** | Global audience, simple | 10% | Good for international |
| **Etsy** | Printable, planner, worksheet | % + listing fee | High organic traffic for printables |
| **Tokopedia** | Physical + digital, Indonesian mass market | % | High volume, lower margins |

## Product-Platform Match

| Product Type | Best Platform |
|-------------|--------------|
| Financial planner (Excel) | Mayar.id, Gumroad |
| Ebook (PDF) | Lynk.id, Mayar.id |
| Printable worksheet | Etsy (global), Lynk.id (local) |
| Planner bundle | Gumroad, Mayar.id |
| Online course | Scalev, Teachable |
| Template (Notion, Figma) | Gumroad, Lynk.id |

## Pricing Guidelines (Indonesia)
| Product | Price Range | Notes |
|---------|------------|-------|
| Simple ebook | Rp 30K–75K | Entry point |
| Planner (PDF) | Rp 50K–150K | Printable premium |
| Excel tracker | Rp 75K–200K | Functional value |
| Bundle (ebook + planner + worksheet) | Rp 150K–300K | Best perceived value |
| Online course | Rp 200K–1.5M | Depends on topic |

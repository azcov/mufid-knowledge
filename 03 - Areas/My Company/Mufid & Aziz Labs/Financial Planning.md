# Financial Planning

## Strategy: Bootstrap First
No VC, no investors at the start. Build revenue from products first.
Reason: products are consumer/SME SaaS — don't need runway, need users.
Indonesian VC typically wants traction anyway before writing a check.

## Financial Milestones

### Milestone 1 — Proof of Concept (Rp 10M MRR)
- At least 1 product with paying users
- Register PT Perorangan
- Open business bank account
- **Action:** quit nothing, keep building others

### Milestone 2 — Side Income Validated (Rp 30M MRR)
- 1–2 products with consistent MRR
- MRR > day job salary supplement (meaningful side income)
- Can afford to outsource design or marketing
- **Action:** hire first contractor (designer or marketing)

### Milestone 3 — Crossover Point (Rp 80–100M MRR)
- MRR approaches or exceeds day job salary
- Can consider reducing day job hours or going full-time
- Upgrade to full PT
- **Action:** evaluate going independent; hire first part-time engineer

### Milestone 4 — Real Company (Rp 200M+ MRR)
- Multiple products generating consistent revenue
- Hire first full-time employee
- Formalize company structure, payroll, BPJS
- **Action:** full-time founder, build team

### Milestone 5 — Growth Stage (Rp 500M+ MRR)
- Consider raising from Indonesian VC if product needs acceleration
- Expand product scope or double down on winners
- Team of 5–10

## Funding Strategy

### Phase 1–2: Fully Bootstrap
- Products are self-funding: build → get users → subscription revenue
- No investors needed for SaaS at this scale
- Keep 100% ownership

### Phase 3 (Optional): Raise if needed for specific acceleration
**Who to approach (Indonesia):**
| Investor | Focus | Typical ticket |
|---------|-------|---------------|
| East Ventures | Indonesian digital SME | $100K–1M |
| Intudo Ventures | Indonesia-first | $500K–5M |
| Prasetia Dwidharma | Indonesian founders | $100K–2M |
| Iterative | SEA SaaS | $150K |
| Y Combinator | Global, remote | $500K |

**When:** Only raise if one product has clear product-market fit (D30 > 35%, MRR growing > 20% MoM) and needs capital to grow faster than organic revenue allows.

## Tax Notes (Indonesia)

| Stage | Tax obligation |
|-------|---------------|
| Personal brand (now) | None (no entity) |
| PT Perorangan | PPh Final 0.5% of gross revenue (if UMKM) |
| Full PT | PPh Badan 22% of net profit |
| Employees hired | PPh 21 (employee income tax), BPJS |
| Revenue > Rp 4.8M/year | Consider PKP (taxable for VAT) |

**Tip:** Use an accountant/bookkeeper from Milestone 1. Monthly cost ~Rp 500K–1M, saves far more in errors and time.

# Scope & Goals — Bengkelterdekat

## Goal
Indonesian auto workshop directory — find the RIGHT workshop by specialization, not just nearest. Car owners discover for free; workshop owners pay Rp 149K/month to manage listings.

## In Scope (MVP = Era 1)
- Database: workshops, specialization_tags, cities, owners, subscriptions
- Admin panel: workshop CRUD, bulk CSV import, tag management
- Public directory: search by city + keyword + filters (specialization, car brand, price range)
- Workshop detail pages (SSR, mobile-responsive, SEO-ready)
- Programmatic SEO landing pages: `/bengkel/[tag]/[city]` (10 cities × 14 tags = 140+ pages)
- Owner claim flow + photo verification
- Xendit payment (Rp 149K/month or Rp 1.49M/year)
- Owner dashboard: edit listing, post promos, view analytics
- AdSense integration (display ads for free visitors)
- 500+ workshop listings at launch (10 cities)
- Structured data (JSON-LD LocalBusiness) for Google rich results

## Out of Scope (MVP)
- Booking / reservation system
- Public listing submission (admin inserts all at MVP)
- Workshop legitimacy verification / license check
- Motorcycle (motor) listings — Phase/Era 2
- Cities outside Top 10 — Era 2
- Mobile app — mobile-responsive web only
- Review/rating system by car owners — Era 2
- Parts/sparepart e-commerce
- Insurance / warranty integration

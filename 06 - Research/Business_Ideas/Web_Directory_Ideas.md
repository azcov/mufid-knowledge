# Web Directory Ideas — Indonesia

Source: "Ide SaaS untuk market Indonesia" (2026-05-02), "Side projects for passive developer income" (2026-04-11)

## Why Directories Work in Indonesia
- Low capital to start (no marketplace complexity)
- Dual monetization: AdSense (traffic) + listing subscription (B2B)
- Indonesia has many segments "belum ter-index" — Google Maps is too generic
- SEO moat builds over time: programmatic pages per city × category

## Directory Monetization Models

| Model | How It Works | Best For |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| AdSense | Display ads on listing pages | High-traffic directories |
| Owner subscription | Listing owner pays to manage/boost listing | Service businesses (bengkel, kontraktor) |
| Lead generation | Charge per qualified inquiry | High-value services (legal, property) |
| Freemium download | Free basic, paid premium | Template/document directories |
| Featured placement | Pay to appear at top of search results | Competitive categories |

## Programmatic SEO Structure
Standard pattern for Indonesian directories:
```
/[category]/[city]              → e.g., /bengkel/surabaya
/[category]/[tag]/[city]        → e.g., /bengkel/kaki-kaki/surabaya
/[entity-slug]/[city]           → e.g., /sekolah/jakarta-selatan
```
Each page = one indexable SEO entry point.
10 cities × 14 tags = 140+ pages on day 1.

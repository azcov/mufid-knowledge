# Competitor Analysis — Sekolah-Dir

| Competitor | Weakness | Our Edge |
|------------|----------|----------|
| **School websites** | Marketing brochures, inconsistent data, many schools have none | Structured, comparable, searchable |
| **Facebook parent groups** | Subjective, unstructured, not searchable | Filterable by curriculum, fee range, accreditation |
| **Word of mouth** | Limited sample size, often outdated | 300K+ schools, verified data |
| **Mamikos** | Rental marketplace — different vertical | School directory — different product |
| **Zenius/Ruangguru** | EdTech / learning platforms | School discovery, not content |

## Market Context
Indonesia has 300,000+ schools. Parents relocating to new city/neighborhood have no structured way to discover what's available.
Key filter parents want: curriculum type (Kurikulum Merdeka vs Cambridge vs IB vs pesantren), fee range, religious identity — no existing tool supports all simultaneously.

## Monetization Risk
Schools may resist paying if they get organic traffic from the free tier.
Counter: Premium schools get **qualified leads** (parents who filled a form) — directly attributable ROI, not just views.

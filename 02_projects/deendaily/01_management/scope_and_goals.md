# Scope & Goals — DeenDaily

## Goal
Muslim ibadah habit tracker — daily check-in for salah, sunnah practices, and personal goals. Mobile-first (React Native), freemium B2C. Global market, primary: Indonesia.

## In Scope (MVP)
- Daily check-in screen (< 30 seconds to complete)
- Salah tracking (5 prayers: on-time / qadha / missed)
- Preset sunnah habits (Quran pages, morning/evening adhkar, sunnah fast)
- Custom habit tracking (free: up to 3; paid: unlimited)
- 7-day streak view (free tier shows 7 days only)
- Streak counter (consecutive-day check-in)
- Auth: email + Google OAuth + Apple Sign-In (iOS App Store required)
- In-app purchase (IAP) — App Store + Play Store
- Push notifications (daily check-in reminder)
- English + Bahasa Indonesia (locale-based)
- iOS + Android (React Native / Expo)
- Backend: Go + Gin + PostgreSQL (Supabase via Neon Auth)
- Deployed on Render.com (Singapore)

## Out of Scope (MVP)
- Community / halaqah accountability groups
- Quran reader, audio recitation, tajwid
- Prayer times / qibla direction
- Islamic content feed (hadith, articles)
- B2B (mosques, Islamic schools)
- Web app
- Multi-language beyond English + Bahasa Indonesia

# Design Tokens — Invoice App

## Brand Personality
**Professional · Clean · Fast · Built for Indonesian Creatives**

## Colors
| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Primary | `#7C3AED` | Violet — creative, modern, premium |
| Accent | `#F59E0B` | Amber — money, action |
| Paid | `#10B981` | Green — ✅ invoice paid |
| Pending | `#F59E0B` | Amber — ⏳ awaiting payment |
| Overdue | `#EF4444` | Red — ❌ overdue |
| Surface | `#FAFAFA` | Clean white |
| Dark | `#18181B` | Text |

## Typography
| Role | Font | Weight |
|------|------|--------|
| Headline | Inter | 800 |
| Body | Inter | 400–500 |
| Invoice number | JetBrains Mono | 500 |
| Amount | Inter | 700 |

## Visual Style
- **Style:** Clean, minimal, modern. Invoice card previews as hero visual.
- **Payment method badges:** QRIS, GoPay, OVO, Dana — always visible
- **Status colors:** Green/amber/red for invoice status — clear at a glance
- **Avoid:** Too corporate — this is for creative freelancers, not enterprise

## Brand Voice
- Understands freelancer life ("Tagih klien itu tidak harus awkward")
- Empowers, not lectures
- Practical and direct ("60 detik, invoice selesai, klien bisa langsung bayar")
- Zero subscription angle: "Kamu bayar kalau dibayar — tidak ada yang lain"

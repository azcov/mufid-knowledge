# Roadmap — Invoice App

## Phase 1: MVP — Q3–Q4 2026
Core invoicing, client payment portal, all Indonesian payment methods, status tracking, auto-reminders, transaction fee collection

Gate: 1,000 active users, 5,000 paid invoices/month, conversion ≥ 60%, MRR equivalent from fees ≥ Rp 15M

## Phase 2: Retention — Q1–Q2 2027
Invoice templates, multi-currency (USD/SGD), team/multi-user, payment analytics, recurring invoices

## Phase 3: Ecosystem — Q3 2027
Accounting integrations, mobile app, e-Meterai (legal requirement for Indonesian contracts > Rp 5M)

## Future
API access (freelancers building their own billing tools)
White-label for Indonesian banks / fintech partners

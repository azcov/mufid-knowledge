# Roadmap — Sekolah-Dir

## Phase 1: MVP — Q3–Q4 2026
Parent search + listing pages + review system + school subscription
Data seeding from NPSN/Dapodik + manual enrichment of top 1,000 schools

Gate: 50K parent sessions, 1,000+ listings, 70 paying schools, MRR ≥ Rp 9.93M

## Phase 2: Trust & Growth — Q1–Q2 2027
Review responses by schools, verified parent badge, programmatic SEO pages
("SD Terbaik di Jakarta Selatan"), school open house announcements, AdSense optimization
Expand to 20 cities

Gate: 200K sessions, 300 paying schools, MRR ≥ Rp 45M

## Phase 3: Online Admission — Q3–Q4 2027
Online admission inquiry pipeline, enrollment season tools, premium school landing pages

## Future
School comparison tool (side-by-side structured fields)
Integration with Kemendikbud accreditation data (live refresh)

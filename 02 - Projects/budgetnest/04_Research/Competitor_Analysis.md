# Competitor Analysis — BudgetNest

## Direct Competitors
| Competitor | Strengths | Weaknesses | Our Advantage |
|------------|-----------|------------|---------------|
| **YNAB** | Powerful budgeting methodology | USD, English, $99/yr, no Indonesian context | IDR, Bahasa, Rp 29K/mo, shared household |
| **Mint** | Free, US integrations | US-only, discontinued | Built for Indonesia, active |
| **Money Manager** | Available in Indonesia | No shared household, complex UI | Shared ledger is the core product |
| **Spendee** | Clean UX, shared wallets | No Indonesian banks, EUR pricing | Local pricing, IDR default |
| **Google Sheets** | Free, flexible | Breaks with 2 users, no automation | Real-time sync, automatic calculations |

## Indonesian Market
- No dominant Indonesian-built household budget tool
- WhatsApp groups are the primary "workaround" — shows strong demand for shared tracking
- Couples aged 25–40 with smartphones are primary target — underserved

## Key Differentiators
1. Shared household ledger at Rp 29K/mo (cheapest option that actually works for 2 people)
2. Full Bahasa Indonesia — no translation awkwardness
3. Built around Indonesian payment habits (no bank sync needed — manual is acceptable here)
4. AI receipt scan — differentiator for tech-savvy users

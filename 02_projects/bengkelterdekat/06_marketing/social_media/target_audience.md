# Target Audience — Bengkelterdekat

## Primary — Car Owners (Visitors, B2C)
| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Age | 25–45 |
| Gender | 70% male |
| Location | Jakarta, Surabaya, Bandung, Medan, Semarang |
| Income | Middle class (Rp 5–20M/month) |
| Car type | Japanese (Toyota, Honda, Suzuki), Korean growing fast |
| Behavior | Googles when car has a problem, asks in Facebook/WhatsApp groups |
| Pain | Can't find workshop that handles their specific issue |
| Trigger | Strange noise, warning light, scheduled service |

## Secondary — Workshop Owners (B2B-lite)
| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Age | 30–55 |
| Business | Independent bengkel, 3–15 mechanics |
| Pain | Customers only find them by word of mouth or Google Maps luck |
| Goal | Get discovered by the right customer, not just the nearest one |
| Social behavior | Uses WhatsApp Business, has Facebook page (often inactive) |

## Psychographics (Car Owners)
- Anxious when car breaks down — needs to trust the place they take it
- Frustrated by: quoted one price, charged another
- Values: expertise over proximity, transparency over charm
- Research behavior: reads Google Maps reviews, asks "ada yang tau..." in WA groups

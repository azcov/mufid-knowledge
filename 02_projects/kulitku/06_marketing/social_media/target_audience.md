# Target Audience — Kulitku

## Primary — Pregnant & Breastfeeding Mothers
| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Age | 22–35 |
| Status | Hamil atau menyusui, sangat careful dengan produk yang dipakai |
| Pain | Tidak tahu mana skincare yang aman, takut salah pilih |
| Trigger | Baru tahu hamil, atau baca artikel tentang bahaya skincare saat hamil |
| Behavior | Aktif di grup FB/WA ibu hamil, rajin research di Google |
| Keyword searched | "skincare aman untuk ibu hamil", "bahan yang dilarang saat hamil" |

## Secondary — Halal-Conscious Consumers
| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Age | 20–40 |
| Trigger | BPJPH halal certification deadline Oktober 2026 — awareness naik |
| Pain | Tidak tahu cara cek status halal produk skincare |
| Behavior | Muslim, cek halal MUI untuk makanan, mulai extend ke personal care |

## Secondary — Parents (Children's Products)
| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Age | 28–42 |
| Pain | Ingin pastikan produk anak bebas bahan berbahaya |
| Trigger | Baca kasus produk bayi dengan bahan berbahaya |

## Psychographics
- Research-heavy before buying any product when pregnant
- Trusts science and data over marketing claims
- Influenced by: bidan, dokter kandungan content, sesama ibu di komunitas
- Skeptical of brand claims — wants third-party verification
- The halal check and BPOM verification are emotional safety anchors

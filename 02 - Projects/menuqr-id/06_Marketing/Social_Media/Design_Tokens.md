# Design Tokens — MenuQR

## Brand Personality
**Warm · Appetizing · Simple · For Every Warung**
Feels like the helpful neighbor who shows your warung how to go digital.

## Colors
| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Primary | `#EA580C` | Warm orange — food, energy, appetite |
| Secondary | `#FBBF24` | Amber yellow — warmth, Indonesian |
| Dark | `#1C1917` | Near-black for text |
| Surface | `#FFF7ED` | Warm cream background |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Clean |

## Typography
| Role | Font | Weight |
|------|------|--------|
| Headline | Plus Jakarta Sans | 800 |
| Body | Plus Jakarta Sans | 400–500 |
| Menu items (demo) | Inter | 500 |

## Visual Style
- **Photography:** Real warung and kafe interiors, Indonesian food, QR code on table
- **Style:** Warm, inviting, food-forward. Never cold or corporate.
- **Graphics:** Orange + warm cream. Bold headlines. Food emoji OK.
- **Avoid:** Western cafe aesthetics — this is for Indonesian warung culture

## Brand Voice
- Friendly and encouraging ("Warungmu bisa lebih kekinian!")
- Simple language — warung owners are not tech people
- Show don't tell — always demo, never just describe
- Empowering: "Kamu bisa setup ini sendiri"

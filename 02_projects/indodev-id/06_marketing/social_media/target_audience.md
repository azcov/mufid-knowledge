# Target Audience — IndoDev-ID

## Side A — Developers (Supply)
| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Age | 22–35 |
| Role | Frontend, Backend, Fullstack, Mobile, Data Engineer, Designer, PM |
| Pain | Glints/Kalibrr noisy — mixed with non-tech jobs. Salary hidden. |
| Goal | Find tech-only roles with salary ranges visible before applying |
| Behavior | Active on Twitter/X tech community, GitHub, LinkedIn |

## Side B — Companies (Demand)
| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Type | Indonesian startups, tech companies, global remote-first companies |
| Pain | LinkedIn expensive, Glints delivers unqualified applicants |
| Goal | Reach qualified Indonesian tech talent without noise |
| Behavior | LinkedIn, startup Slack communities, Telegram channels |

## Psychographics (Developers)
- Tired of "competitive salary" — wants real numbers before wasting time applying
- Proud of their craft — responds to content that respects tech as a profession
- Community-oriented: follows #devIndonesia, joins Telegram Go/React communities
- Skeptical of job boards — needs to trust IndoDev-ID has real, quality listings

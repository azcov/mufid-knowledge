# Content Types — Bengkelterdekat

## Pillar 1 — Education (40%)
> "Teach car owners what to ask for"

| Format | Example |
|--------|---------|
| Carousel | "5 spesialisasi bengkel yang harus kamu tau" |
| Reels | "Bedanya bengkel umum vs spesialis kaki-kaki" |
| Infographic | "Ciri-ciri bengkel AC mobil yang kompeten" |
| Tips | "Kenapa mobilmu bunyi begini → bengkel apa yang harus didatangi" |

## Pillar 2 — Discovery / Problem-Solution (35%)
> "Show the gap Bengkelterdekat fills"

| Format | Example |
|--------|---------|
| Before/After | "Dulu cari bengkel: Google Maps → tebak-tebakan. Sekarang: filter spesialisasi" |
| Reels demo | Screen recording: search by tag, find specialist, open WhatsApp |
| Testimonial | Workshop owner: "Sejak listing di sini, customer lebih tepat sasaran" |
| Problem post | "Pernah bawa mobil ke bengkel yang salah? Ini solusinya" |

## Pillar 3 — Workshop Spotlight (25%)
> "Build trust by featuring quality workshops"

| Format | Example |
|--------|---------|
| Workshop profile | "Bengkel kaki-kaki terpercaya di Bandung: [Nama Bengkel]" |
| Behind the scenes | Video montage: mechanics at work, specialized tools |
| "Bengkel of the week" | Highlight 1 workshop per week with their specialization |

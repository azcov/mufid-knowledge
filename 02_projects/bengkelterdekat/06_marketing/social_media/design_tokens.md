# Design Tokens — Bengkelterdekat

## Brand Personality
**Trustworthy · Expert · No-nonsense · Indonesian**
Think: a mechanic who knows his stuff and gives you a straight answer.

## Colors
| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Primary | `#1A2332` | Dark navy — trust, expertise |
| Accent | `#F97316` | Orange — energy, automotive |
| Surface | `#F8F9FA` | Light gray — clean background |
| Text | `#111827` | Near-black |
| Muted | `#6B7280` | Secondary text |

## Typography
| Role | Font | Weight |
|------|------|--------|
| Headline | Inter or Plus Jakarta Sans | 700–800 |
| Body | Inter | 400–500 |
| Caption | Inter | 400 |

## Visual Style
- **Photography:** Real workshop photos — tools, engines, mechanics at work. Authentic, not stock.
- **Graphics:** Clean, bold text overlays on dark backgrounds. Orange CTA buttons.
- **Icons:** Line-style automotive icons (wrench, gear, car silhouette)
- **Avoid:** Generic stock photos of smiling people at a desk

## Social Media Templates
- Feed post: 1:1 (1080×1080px)
- Stories: 9:16 (1080×1920px)
- Carousel: 1:1, max 7 slides
- Reels cover: 9:16

## Brand Voice
- Direct and confident ("Cari bengkel yang tepat, bukan yang terdekat")
- Educational, not salesy
- Indonesian language — Bahasa Indonesia formal tapi bukan kaku

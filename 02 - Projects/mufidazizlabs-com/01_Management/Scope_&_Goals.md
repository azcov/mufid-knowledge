# Scope & Goals — Mufid & Aziz Labs

## What It Is Now
A branding layer alongside Mufid's real name. All products are side projects built during evenings/weekends — nothing released to users yet.

## What It Will Become
A real product company with employees, once one or more products gain traction and generate enough revenue to justify it.

## The Website Goal
- Land the brand: show who Mufid & Aziz Labs is and what's being built
- Portfolio page: list all products in development
- Signal seriousness to future users, collaborators, or hires

## Stack
React + TypeScript + Vite + Tailwind CSS · Deployed on Cloudflare Pages

# Free SEO & Keyword Research Tools

Source: "Situs gratis untuk riset SEO dan keyword" (2026-05-27)

## 100% Free (No Limits)

| Tool | Link | Fitur Utama |
|------|------|-------------|
| **Google Keyword Planner** | ads.google.com/tools/keyword-planner | Volume, CPC, kompetisi — data langsung dari Google |
| **Google Trends** | trends.google.com | Tren keyword, musiman, regional Indonesia |
| **Google Search Console** | search.google.com/search-console | Keywords yang sudah ranking (hanya untuk domain sendiri) |
| **Answer the Public** | answerthepublic.com | Question-based keywords (3 searches/day free) |

## Free with Limits

| Tool | Limit | Keunggulan |
|------|-------|-----------|
| **Ubersuggest** | 3 searches/day | Keyword ideas + competitor analysis |
| **SEMrush** | 10 queries/day | Industry-leading data, limited free |
| **Ahrefs Webmaster** | Free for own site | Backlink + keyword for own domain |
| **Keyword Surfer** (Chrome extension) | Unlimited | Volume muncul langsung di Google SERP |
| **Moz Keyword Explorer** | 10 queries/month | Authority metrics |

## Indonesian-Specific Tools

| Tool | Notes |
|------|-------|
| **Google Trends** (set region: Indonesia) | Best for understanding Indonesian search behavior |
| **Google Keyword Planner** (set language: Bahasa Indonesia) | Most accurate for local volume |
| **Facebook Audience Insights** | Understand Indonesian demographics + interests |

## SEO Strategy for Indonesian SaaS
1. Target Bahasa Indonesia keywords first ("aplikasi manajemen kos", "slip gaji online")
2. Low competition + high intent queries are still plentiful in Indonesia
3. Google Trends: compare "slip gaji" vs "payslip" → Bahasa dominates
4. Long-tail Indonesian queries have very little SEO competition vs English equivalents

## AI & AdSense Impact
Source: "AI agents and AdSense revenue impact" (2026-05-29)

- AI-generated content can impact AdSense approval if detected as low quality
- Human-edited AI content generally acceptable
- Focus on original research, local Indonesian data → harder to replicate by AI
- High-quality directory content (structured, verified data) → strong AdSense RPM

# Design Tokens — BudgetNest

## Brand Personality
**Warm · Together · Honest · Modern Indonesian**
Feels like advice from a smart friend — not a bank.

## Colors
| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Primary | `#10B981` | Emerald green — growth, money, positive |
| Secondary | `#F59E0B` | Amber — warmth, optimism |
| Surface | `#FFFFFF` | Clean white |
| Dark | `#1F2937` | Text, dark mode |
| Soft | `#ECFDF5` | Light green background |

## Typography
| Role | Font | Weight |
|------|------|--------|
| Headline | Plus Jakarta Sans | 700 |
| Body | Plus Jakarta Sans | 400–500 |
| Numbers | Inter | 600 (for financial figures) |

## Visual Style
- **Aesthetic:** Clean, minimal, couple-focused. Warm photos of couples at home, on phones together.
- **Graphics:** Soft green + white. Round cards, soft shadows.
- **Illustrations:** Simple flat finance icons (wallet, chart, house, baby)
- **Avoid:** Stock photos of men in suits. No corporate feel.

## Brand Voice
- Conversational and warm ("Yuk, atur keuangan bareng")
- Honest about money being stressful (not pretending it's easy)
- Indonesian Gen Z/Millennial language
- Light humor OK — money doesn't have to be scary

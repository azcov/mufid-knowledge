# Scope & Goals — Sekolah-Dir

## Goal
Indonesian school directory and review platform. Parents find and compare schools (Playgroup → SMA) for free. Schools pay for verified profiles, priority placement, and admission inquiry leads.

## In Scope (MVP — Q3–Q4 2026)
**Parent side (free):** Search by city/district/level/curriculum/fee range, school listing pages, comparison shortlist (up to 5 schools), parent review system (OTP-verified), proximity search, Bahasa Indonesia + English toggle.

**School side (paid):**
- Free: basic listing (name, address, level, type — can't edit own data)
- Basic (Rp 99K/mo): verified badge, self-manage profile + photos, priority over free listings
- Premium (Rp 249K/mo): featured placement, admission inquiry leads, analytics, promo banner

**Data Seeding:** Import from NPSN/Dapodik (300K+ schools), manually enrich top 1,000 in 10 cities.

## Out of Scope (MVP)
- School management SaaS (attendance, grades, LMS) — different product
- Tutor / les privat marketplace
- Parent community / forum (Phase 2)
- Online admission pipeline (Phase 3)
- Non-Indonesia schools
